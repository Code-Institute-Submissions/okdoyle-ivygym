Welcome to Iveagh Gym

At Iveagh gym we realise that each of our members has a different reason for joining.
Whatever your reason may be, our team is here to help you to achieve your goals & realise your full potential.

TRAIN

Our club is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals. We have created a club with high ceilings, lots of natural light and plenty of space so you can enjoy your workout. Our expert Fitness Team is here to help.

SPIN

Indoor group cycling class. Simulates cycling on the road by using various sitting & standing positions whilst changing resistance via a flywheel. Great for Fitness, Strength & Weight Loss.

SWIM

We are one of a select few fitness facilities, which offers a swimming pool in the heart of Dublin. Whether it is for specific fitness training or just a relaxing swim, the Iveagh pool can accommodate your needs.
